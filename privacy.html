Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2, 2026

Summary: Court Stream Clipper is an automated service that processes publicly available video content. We do not collect personal information from end users who view our posted content. This policy describes how we handle data during our automated processing.

1. Information We Process

1.1 Source Video Data

The Service processes:

  • Public video streams: Publicly available YouTube videos and live streams
  • Video metadata: Titles, descriptions, upload dates, channel information
  • Audio transcription: Automated speech-to-text analysis to detect keywords
  • Video analysis: Automated detection of audio levels and visual content

1.2 Platform Authentication Data

To upload content to social media platforms, we securely store:

  • OAuth tokens for TikTok and YouTube APIs
  • API credentials (client keys and secrets)
  • Platform-specific upload metadata

This data is stored locally on our servers and is never shared with third parties.

1.3 Processing Database

We maintain a local database that tracks:

  • URLs of processed videos
  • Timestamps of detected events
  • Keywords and triggers identified
  • Clips created and upload status
  • Platform video IDs and URLs

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Court Stream Clipper does not collect:

  • Personal information from viewers of our content
  • IP addresses or device information from end users
  • Cookies or tracking data from website visitors
  • User accounts or login credentials (except platform API tokens)
  • Behavioral data or analytics from viewers
  • Email addresses or contact information (except for service operation)

3. How We Use Information

Information processed by the Service is used solely to:

  • Identify and extract clips from public video sources
  • Create short-form video content
  • Upload clips to social media platforms (TikTok, YouTube)
  • Track processing status and prevent duplicate uploads
  • Maintain service functionality and reliability

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All data is stored on our private servers and is not transmitted to third parties except:

  • Video uploads to TikTok and YouTube (via their official APIs)
  • Source video downloads from YouTube (via yt-dlp)

4.2 Security Measures

We implement security measures including:

  • Encrypted API credentials and OAuth tokens
  • Restricted file system permissions
  • Secure environment variable management
  • Regular automated cleanup of temporary files

4.3 Data Retention

  • Source videos: Automatically deleted after processing (configurable)
  • Clips: Retained for 30 days then deleted (configurable)
  • Database records: Retained indefinitely for service operation
  • OAuth tokens: Retained until revoked or service discontinued

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Platforms We Interact With

The Service interacts with these third-party platforms:

  • YouTube: For source content and upload destination
  • TikTok: For upload destination

Each platform has its own privacy policy governing how they handle uploaded content.

5.2 Public Content

Content posted by the Service to TikTok and YouTube is public and subject to those platforms' privacy policies. We recommend reviewing:

  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • YouTube/Google Privacy Policy

6. AI and Automated Processing

The Service uses artificial intelligence (OpenAI Whisper) for speech-to-text transcription to detect keywords. This processing:

  • Occurs entirely on our local servers
  • Does not send audio data to external AI services
  • Is used only to identify moments of interest in public videos
  • Does not create profiles or track individuals

7. Children's Privacy

The Service does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The Service processes publicly available content that may be viewed by all ages on third-party platforms, subject to those platforms' policies.

8. Your Rights

8.1 Content Removal

If you believe content posted by the Service contains your personal information or infringes your rights, you may:

  • Contact us at: funnycourtclips@gmail.com
  • Request removal from TikTok or YouTube directly using platform reporting tools

8.2 Data Access

Since we do not collect personal information from viewers, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. Source content is already public.

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect. As stated above, we do not collect personal information from end users viewing our content. The service only processes publicly available video content.

10. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area:

  • We process only publicly available data
  • We do not collect personal data from individuals viewing our content
  • Source video content is obtained from public YouTube channels
  • Uploaded content is subject to the privacy policies of TikTok and YouTube

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when changes were last made. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
